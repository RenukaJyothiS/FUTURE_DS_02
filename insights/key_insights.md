# 📊 Business Insights

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes customer retention and churn patterns for a subscription-based telecommunications company. Using **Python** for data cleaning and analysis and **Power BI** for dashboard creation, several key factors influencing customer churn were identified.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📉 Customer Churn Rate

* 🔸 **26.58%** of customers have churned.
* 🔸 **73.42%** of customers have been retained.
* 🔸 More than **1 in 4 customers** leave the service, highlighting the need for effective retention strategies.

---

### 📑 Contract Type Analysis

* 📌 Customers with **Month-to-Month** contracts have the highest churn.
* 📌 Customers with **One-Year** and **Two-Year** contracts are significantly more loyal.
* 💡 Promoting long-term contracts can help reduce churn.

---

### 🌐 Internet Service Analysis

* 📡 Customers using **Fiber Optic** internet services experience higher churn than DSL users.
* 📈 This suggests potential improvements in pricing, service quality, or customer experience.

---

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis

* 💰 Customers paying through **Electronic Check** show the highest churn.
* ✅ Customers using **automatic payment methods** tend to stay longer.
* 💡 Encouraging automatic payments may improve customer retention.

---

### ⏳ Customer Lifetime Analysis

* 📅 Customers within their **first 12 months** have the highest churn.
* 📈 Churn decreases as customer tenure increases.
* ❤️ Long-term customers demonstrate stronger loyalty.

---

### 💵 Monthly Charges Analysis

* 📊 Customers paying **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn.
* 💡 Personalized pricing strategies and targeted offers can improve retention.

---

### 👵 Senior Citizen Analysis

* 👥 The **Senior Citizen** customer segment contributes to churn.
* 🤝 Providing dedicated support and simplified service plans may improve retention for this group.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

* 🎯 Encourage customers to switch to **annual or two-year contracts** through discounts and loyalty offers.
* 🚀 Strengthen customer onboarding during the **first year** to reduce early churn.
* 🌐 Improve **Fiber Optic** service quality and customer support.
* 💳 Promote **automatic payment methods** by offering small incentives.
* 🎁 Introduce **loyalty rewards** for long-term customers.
* 📢 Identify high-risk customers early and provide personalized retention offers before they churn.

---

# 🏁 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that **contract type**, **customer tenure**, **internet service**, **payment method**, and **monthly charges** are the primary drivers of customer churn.

By implementing targeted retention strategies based on these insights, subscription-based businesses can improve customer satisfaction, increase retention, and reduce revenue loss through data-driven decision-making.
