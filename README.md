# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important factors for the success of subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer churn patterns using the **Telco Customer Churn Dataset** to identify the key drivers of customer loss, understand customer lifetime trends, and provide actionable recommendations to improve retention.

The analysis was performed using **Python** for data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA), while **Power BI** was used to build an interactive dashboard for business storytelling and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer churn and retention trends.
* Identify the major factors influencing customer churn.
* Perform customer lifetime and tenure analysis.
* Conduct tenure-based cohort analysis.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Provide business recommendations to reduce customer churn.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset Used:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, subscription details, service usage, billing information, contract types, payment methods, customer tenure, and churn status.

### Dataset Features

* Customer Demographics
* Contract Type
* Internet Service
* Payment Method
* Monthly Charges
* Total Charges
* Customer Tenure
* Churn Status

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📉 Overall customer churn rate is **26.58%**, while the retention rate is **73.42%**.
* 📑 Customers with **Month-to-Month** contracts experience the highest churn.
* 🌐 **Fiber Optic** customers churn more frequently than DSL customers.
* 💳 Customers using **Electronic Check** have a higher churn rate.
* ⏳ Customers within their **first 12 months** are most likely to leave.
* 💵 Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.
* 👵 Senior Citizen customers represent an important segment for targeted retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 🎯 Promote long-term contracts through attractive discounts.
* 🚀 Strengthen customer onboarding during the first year.
* 🌐 Improve Fiber Optic service quality and customer support.
* 💳 Encourage automatic payment methods.
* 🎁 Introduce loyalty and reward programs for long-term customers.
* 📢 Develop proactive retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
